Crater Lake

A lake formed in volcanic crater from the remnants of Mount Mazama. Crater lake is renowned for its beauty, and the vibrant deep blue and clarity of its water.

Haystack Rock

Rising from the waves of the Pacific Ocean, a monolith protrudes over 238 ft from the water. Haystack Rock is home to many marine wildlife.

Mount Hood

An active volcano lying on the Cascade Volcanic Arc. Mt. Hood offers year round skiing and is surrounded by over 1200 miles of hiking trails.
